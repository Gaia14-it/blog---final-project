Everything you should know about the next MET Gala

The Met Gala 2025 is just around the corner, and fashion enthusiasts are eagerly awaiting the biggest night in the fashion world. Here's what you need to know about the upcoming event:

When and Where

The Met Gala 2025 will take place on Monday, May 5, 2025, at the Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York City, located on Fifth Avenue.

Theme

This year's theme is "Superfine: Tailoring Black Style," celebrating Black Dandyism and its historical roots and modern influence through fashion. The theme draws inspiration from Monica L. Miller's 2009 book, "Slaves to Fashion: Black Dandyism and the Styling of Black Diasporic Identity.

Exhibition

The Costume Institute's "Superfine: Tailoring Black Style" exhibition will open to the public on May 6, 2025, and run through October 26, 2025.

Co-Chairs

The 2025 Met Gala will make history with an all-Black slate of co-chairs, alongside Anna Wintour. While the exact list of co-chairs isn't specified here, the announcement has generated significant excitement.

What to Expect

Given the theme, we can anticipate stunning interpretations of tailored Black style on the red carpet, showcasing the creativity and elegance of Black fashion. The event will mark the opening of the Costume Institute's spring exhibition and will feature A-list celebrities in remarkable fashion looks.

The Met Gala 2025 promises to be an unforgettable night of fashion, art, and celebration, and we can't wait to see what the stars bring to the steps of the Metropolitan Museum of Art.
