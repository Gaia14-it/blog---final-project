The guide you need to built a capsule closet

To build a stylish and versatile capsule wardrobe for 2025, consider including these essential pieces:

Tops

  • Simple White Shirt: A classic white shirt with a twist, perfect for dressing up or down
  • Preppy Cardigan: Adds a touch of sophistication to any outfit
  • Scarf Blouse: Elegant and versatile for various occasions
  • Boxy Tees: Relaxed fit for effortless style
  • Oversized Button-Downs: Versatile for both casual and dressy events

Bottoms

  • Baggy Jeans: Comfortable and trendy, perfect for everyday wear
  • Tailored Trousers: Sophisticated and versatile for formal events
  • Skirt: Timeless and chic for professional settings

Dresses

  • Simple Dress: A wardrobe staple for easy, elegant outfits

Outerwear

  • Wax Jacket: Waterproof and stylish for rainy days
  • Oversized Outerwear: Includes blazers, trench coats, and puffer jackets for comfort and style
  • Tailored Blazer: Adds sophistication to any outfit

Footwear

  • Sneakers: Comfortable and perfect for any casual outfit
  • Heels: A nice staple for when you have to dress elegant
  • Boots: The perfect item for the rainy days and to add something to your outfit
